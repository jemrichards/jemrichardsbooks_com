Poisoned Rose

Synapsis:
Central Europe- 1327
Medieval Times:

Nikkita is a peasant that lives deep in the country. Both her parents were killed when she was only 16 by outlaws, now she is on her own. A traumatic event had left her with a child to care for, and she fights to survive during rough times doing anything she can to forge a life for her and her child. Nikkita does not trust men since her traumatic experience, she has found ways to protect herself and keep at a distance, but when she finds a man dying in the woods she knows she must help him.

Adrian is a prince passing through the country alone when he has a horrible accident. Nikkita finds him unconscious and badly injured; she takes him home and nurses him. She is determined to help him, but also to keep her distance out of mistrust of men. The Prince falls in love and has to work hard to charm and win her over.  

Wild Heart Of The Sea: Birth of the Avenging Angel
Book 1 of Wild Heart Series

Synapsis:

Crystal Boudier is the oldest Governor’s daughter of Port Royal, Jamaica. All her life she has had a stifling life filled with lessons and responsibilities. But one day, pirates raid her city to pillage and plunder. Crystal and a handful of her friends manage to assist people to a ship in the harbor and sail to safety. Upon their return, Crystal finds her parents dead and her sister was taken hostage by the fearsome pirates.

Crystal takes up the mission to fight pirates and assist the cities people in their times of need in her search for her beloved sister. Will she be able to make a difference in a world where pirates run the seas and strike fear in the people’s hearts? Will she manage to find her little sister and bring her to safety? 

Wild Heart Of The Seas: Give No Quarter
Book 2 of Wild Heart Series

Synapsis:

Join Crystal and the rest of the crew in the second book of the Wild Heart of the Seas series! Crystal sends Captain Stevenson and the Saving Grace to patrol Eastern Africa and Indonesia while she guides the Avenging Angel around the Caribbean and Western Africa.

Get ready to discover where their travels take them and the adventures they have! Crystal finally discovers her true feelings for Commodore Sullivan, but she isn’t sure where it will lead her! In the meantime, Crystal is just as heroic and sassy as ever as they continue in their mission. They make new friends, face a dangerous storm, and take action to a whole new level! Will Crystal have what it takes to face the dangers yet to come?  

Chosen
Book 1 of Chosen Series

Synapsis:

Raven is Quileute Indian with her people’s wolf shape-shifting ability. However, that’s not all! Raven also has the ability to shape-shift into any animal she wishes. When life for her people changes forever and a very real threat becomes evident to all of the Quileute tribe, Raven is the ‘chosen one’ by the Great Spirit and Earth Mother. She is gifted with a strong connection to the spirit realm to receive visions among other gifts, and Earth Mother grants her with earth magic over earth elements.However, when things heat up and Raven struggles with her abilities, she struggles to find the balance while leading the wolf pack through some very tough decisions.

Raven must find it in herself to discover what it will take to become the true leader she was meant to be and use her abilities as she learns to tip the odds in her favor. With the whole tribe counting on her, she feels the heavy burden as she searches for her best defense against the coming threats of vampires. With terrifying visions and new abilities popping up when she least expects them, can Raven learn enough to protect her people?

Gathering
Book 2 of Chosen Series

Synapsis:

Raven strives to obtain a treaty with the vampire council, but rogue vampires arise to create an uprising. As Raven is forced to divide her attention with the rogue vampires and new threats that continuously arise, she must also strive to build relations among the other shifter clans.

As she continues to grow more powerful with her earth magic and makes new discoveries of her people’s history, Raven must face multiple trials along the way. New friendships arise in the most surprising of individuals.

Magik
Book 3 of the Chosen Series

Synapsis:

Sought by evil warlocks, Raven must find it in herself to make a stand and free the innocents’ suffering. Exciting new adventures lie around every corner as Raven tries to improve relations with neighboring tribes in Western Washington and travels to faraway lands fighting for justice.

Her travels take her far from home to a magical land in which Raven must use her wits to free the innocents and make a stand. With the discovery of the warlocks enslaving creatures, she discovers that she must not only free them, but provide a safe place for them to stay until the danger is over. Can she keep all of them safe and prevent them from fighting?

Wicked Lovely Vampire

Synapsis:

Katerina Stacks (Kat for short) is a talented young woman in photography. She’s a freelance photographer who occasionally writes articles in magazines to go with her pictures. Does photo shoots of nature, events, etc. She lives in a small house in the country with her best friend that she is remodeling on her own.
She is attacked one night by mugger when she was out late in Seattle Washington when she was on an assignment. Badly beaten to the point in which she would have died, she is saved by Adrian Black, a vampire who saves her life by giving her his blood. She is miraculously healed by his blood and they discover a strange connection between them.

Adrian Black is hundreds of years old and displays old ways mannerism though he is kind and considerate, and always a gentleman.

Something wicked lovely this way comes!
